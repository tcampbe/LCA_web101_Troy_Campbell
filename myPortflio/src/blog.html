Crooked Reasoning

The old language meaning of the name Campbell is said to mean "Crooked Mouth". I have Americanized that to represent myself as "Crooked Smile". This is meant to infer a slightly roguish sense of enjoying life. I have chosen the caMel as my personal mascot. I call this the caMpbell caMell. This has an english language reference as well as a reference to "camelCase".
