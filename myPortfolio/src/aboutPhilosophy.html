Philosophy

The family is the root of excellence.

“I desire to learn in order to gain personal knowledge.”

“I desire to earn money in order to provide for my basic needs.”


          
Each of us has an internal list of items that starts something 
            like the above statements. It can be a list of any size, but 
            they all stem from our individual needs and our sense of self 
            preservation. 

          

          
Most of us want to see others do well, but it is your family 
            that first brings out the desire for another person to surpass 
            your accomplishments. It is, for your family, that you first 
            strive to move beyond providing for your basic needs. It is your 
            family that drives you to understand charity. It is charity that 
            drives mankind to better itself. It is the desire to improve the 
            plight of mankind that produces true excellence. Excellence that 
            surpasses the needs, hopes, and dreams of one person. The kind 
            of excellence that combines the items on that internal list to 
            create something greater than the sum of its parts.

          

          
“I wish to excel!”
