Vision

The future is unknown. That does not mean we can not contribute to it. 


        
I want to shape a future of wonders. True wonders, not just 
          flying cars and robot assistants, (I am excitedly, for, both!). 
          I want wonders that provide eyes for the blind, ears for the 
          deaf, ambulation for amputees, and the end of uncured disease and 
          cancers. I want a flying assistant with access to the 
          knowledge of a doctor, a chef, an engineer, and a thousand 
          (or million) other specialties.  I want to end poverty and 
          hunger. I want to colonize mars, or even better some planet 
          no one has heard of yet. I want more and better everything.

        

        
I believe technology is key to all those things and my 
          vision is to learn and build, everyday, towards that future 
          of wonders.
